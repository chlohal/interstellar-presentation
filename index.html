General Relativity

  • Principle of Equivalence: gravity is same as acceleration
  • Mass causes spacetime to curve

General Relativistic Time Dilation

  • Curved spacetime means more "distance" between same points.
  • More "distance" means more percieved time. A gravity-well'd clock runs slower.
  • In Interstellar: addressed as "When I'm [...] traveling near the speed of light, or near a black hole, time's gonna change for me. It's gonna run more slowly

Effect of Special Relativity in Interstellar

  • "Two years to Saturn" (8mo to Mars; gravity slingshot; 14mo Mars-Saturn)
    • 365 * 24 * 60 * 60 * 2 = 63072000s
  • Average distance of Saturn from Earth: 1,450,000,000,000m (1.45 trillion meters)
  • distance / time = average speed of 22989.599 m/s: 0.00007663c
  • γ = 1.0000000029360785
  • The time difference is like ~200ms

The Bulk

  • A dimension is either "compact" or "extensive". "compact" means that points are only in a finite range on that dimension.
  • If a brane's extra dimensions aren't compact, we have something called The Bulk.
  • The Bulk is the broader space: the multiverse (but in math)!
for(let i = 0; i < 20; i++) { let c = [0xB1EDE8,0x643A71,0xFF6978][i%3] let x = Math.random()*10 - 5; let y = Math.random()*10 - 5; let zO = Math.random(); this.addDot([ t=>x - 500/t, t=>Math.cos(t/1000 + zO)*5, t=>y - 500/t ],c,0.25); }
